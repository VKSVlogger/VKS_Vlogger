Hostel Life At Government Polytechnic Purnea

07/12/2022

First Day

On 7th December 2022, my hostel life begins. This was the first time I was staying in a hostel and surprisingly room number I got is 202, which also resembles my birth date. So, from there I find positive vibes.

Experience
Every moment is a precious one. Every day we learned new things. There is freedom eveywhere. No need to ask Permission for going outside.

Holiday
The holiday is the best day there, we go to watch movies, go to the game zone, shopping, enjoying a lot.

Birthday
giving a surprise birthday party at sharp 12:00 am, and after cake cutting ceremony, we all dance till 2:00 am. Enjoying everything.

Room Sharing
Room sharing and gossips, clicking pictures, and also completing assignments, making presentation tirelessly and worring about what happened tomorrow during presentation time, and giving each other hope everything will be fine.

Breakfast
Sometime missing morning breakfast, calling friends to please bring breakfast for me. then planning to order food from zomato, or from near by shop like momos, pizza, burger.
Or suddenly planning to go outside for lunch or dinner.
Everytime clicking pitures when we all frinds together, cracking jokes, making fun of each other.
